★ VENUE / THEME 2019 ★

Eco & rustic with intelligently designed, clean and up to standard facilities while you have a higher quality festival experience.

LUNAR UTOPIA FESTIVAL - HONEY FOR LIFE is excited to bring the tribe an experience of heart-opening, mind expanding and wonder-filled celebrations.

Moon Tribe, we invite you to a community gathering of epic proportions!

We invite you to come and regenerate your chi, retreat from your battle in society, ground and let go ~ chill, celebrate, and become yourself.

We have chosen to host, for you, a tribal gathering at a very unique, sacred and high-vibration area on our planet. Here powerful leylines expand our consciousness just by being in close proximity to them.

Come! Be embraced and hugged by nature herself!

Walk in lush green pastures and feel refreshing summer breezes in the shade of indigenous trees; with a panacea of mountain scenery all around you!

The venue for our Time of Deep Tribal Connection is tucked away between the Most ancient Magalies-Mountains and the Buffelspoort koppies. It is on a crossroads of leylines and is the Home of Honey-keepers! (Outeni means 'Honey keepers' ... the Honey of Life ... Deni or Teni is honey in Khoisan.)

THE EVENT THEME is focused on the Honeybee and it's very intrinsic role within the ecological web of life. These amazing creatures are the magical balance keepers and true pollinators of creation!

Bees are said to come from Venus, our neighbouring planet, because it looks like a yellow pollen ball! We honour them through our gathering by teaching, sharing, empowering, and finally fully enjoying the sweetness that life has to offer. We are celebrating our planetary pollinators!

You will discover the original honey-keepers of the Outeniqua! Honey keeping was a sacred art! It is time to again acknowledge the ancients of this land, and the Mountain Spirits that worked side by side with them.

Tranquil spaces yet filled with fun to make you recharge with joy!

We will recapture this energy and open our hearts once more to the honey we have inside of us!
